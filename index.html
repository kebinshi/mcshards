Introduction

What in the world is MCShards?

MCShards is a free Minecraft hosting service focused on bringing out the best player experiences in Minecraft again. All players are allowed to create a vanilla server instance at no costs. Server settings, plugins, worlds can be changed and edited directly in game, eliminating the need for any external control panels. It's that simple. Plug and play!

Getting Started

How do I begin hosting a shard (server) for my friends and family?

Simply login to play.mcshards.net and type in the command /create to launch your own personal shard. You may upgrade your shard to a better environment and plugin access by purchasing plans in the store.
